About Leaf

My name is Leaf (he/him), I'm an 18-year-old Web and Graphics Designer studying at the University of San Francisco. I love writing in HTML, Tailwind CSS, and JS, sketching in Krita or on paper and making vector graphics in Adobe Illustrator. I'm also a fanatic about keyboard-centric window tiling and the Colemak-DH keyboard layout.
In my free time I enjoy playing Osu!, reading visual novels and manga, and watching mystery and mecha anime. If you are in SF and think we might be friends, let's meet up for coffee or tea sometime! You can reach me on wherever you get your fediverse.
