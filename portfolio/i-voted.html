"I Voted!" sticker

Adobe Illustrator

University of San Francisco

Design Fundamentals

September 16th, 2024

For this assignment I drew the Seattle Link light rail bursting out of a cloud. I think that public rapid transit is incredibly empowering and democratizing, in a way that resonates with the significance of voting. To maintain visual clarity while adding detail, I used a complex gradient for the cloud lighting, to bring additional dimensionality to how the cloud interacts with the rail, and to give it the fluffiness I desired. I hope this visual makes people think about how we can make the world more accessible for those in need, by collectively voting for and investing in the things that help the most vulnerable among us.
