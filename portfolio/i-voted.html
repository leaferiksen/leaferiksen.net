"I Voted!" sticker

Adobe Illustrator

University of San Francisco

Design Fundamentals

September 16th, 2024

For my first project in Illustrator I was assigned to design a voting sticker. Inspired both by my homesickness for Seattle and the democratizing nature of public transit, I drew the Link light rail to represent how voting empowers us.
Always vote for better free transit, wherever you are! It is always worth it to invest in what helps the most disadvantaged among us. If you think you are too good for public transit, it is too underfunded to serve you as well as it should, and you should vote to fund it better!
