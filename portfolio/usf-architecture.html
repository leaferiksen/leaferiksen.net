University of San Francisco

Design Fundamentals

October 16th, 2024

Early on in this project, I took a one-point-perspective photo of my dorm’s hallway that had an immeasurable depth which I found very compelling as my central image. For the others I focused on angles that worked with the hallway's diagonal lines by taking many pictures at oblique and Dutch angles and comparing them until I found ones that aligned best.
I learned that my best vectorizing workflow is very similar to sketching; starting with bigger elements before beginning the details let me avoid feeling around in the dark for where to put things. After completion I noticed my color palette is reminicent of the backrooms, which contributes to the haunted look that I was going for.
